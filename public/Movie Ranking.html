The Best Movies According to Harsh

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Shawshank Redemption

The captivating storytelling, powerful characters, and themes of hope and redemption make it a timeless classic.

The Godfather

Its masterful portrayal of the Mafia, complex characters, and the exploration of power and morality in a criminal underworld make it an iconic cinematic experience.

Inception

Christopher Nolan's mind-bending narrative, coupled with stunning visuals and a brilliant score, creates a unique and thought-provoking movie-watching experience.
